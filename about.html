About Lochlann

The product is executive judgment.

Lochlann Strategies was formed to provide leadership teams with an independent operating perspective before growth decisions become costly commitments.

Michael Maglothin has sat in nearly every chair involved in strategic growth.

Government customer. Requirements owner. Evaluator. Systems engineer. Program manager. Capture leader. Business developer. Executive. Founder. Prime integrator. Partner negotiator.

That range matters because strategic decisions fail at the boundaries between functions. Capture can outrun delivery. Partnerships can look stronger on paper than in execution. Customer enthusiasm can be mistaken for funded demand. Internal advocacy can overwhelm objective qualification.

Lochlann exists to expose those gaps early and help leadership act on them.

Direct

No theater.

The work is built around the actual decision, not a prepackaged consulting methodology or an unnecessary volume of presentation material.

Independent

No internal constituency.

Recommendations are not shaped by ownership of a pipeline, proposal, product, partner, or legacy position.

Accountable

No separation between win and execution.

The promise made during capture is tested against what the organization, solution, and partner team can actually deliver.

The Lochlann Standard

Customer reality. Competitive reality. Delivery reality.

Advice is grounded in what the buyer will fund, what competitors can credibly offer, and what the organization can execute after award.

Clear judgment is most valuable before momentum becomes a substitute for evidence.
