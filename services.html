Advisory Areas

Support for the decisions that determine growth—and whether the organization can deliver it.

Lochlann works across opportunity selection, capture, market entry, fractional growth leadership, post-award transition, and recovery.

01

Executive Opportunity Assessment

An independent decision review before leadership commits disproportionate money, time, reputation, or delivery capacity.

  • Customer need, authority, access, and funding
  • Competitive position and incumbency
  • Win probability and investment discipline
  • Partner, solution, price, and execution exposure
  • Bid, no-bid, or conditional-pursuit recommendation
02

Capture Strategy

Executive leadership for pursuits that require more than proposal management.

  • Qualification and capture-plan challenge
  • Customer engagement and positioning
  • Discriminators and competitive strategy
  • Teaming, workshare, and partner governance
  • Price-to-win logic and proposal direction
03

Market Entry

A practical route into federal, regulated, critical-infrastructure, or unfamiliar commercial markets.

  • Market attractiveness and addressable demand
  • Buyer, channel, and partner mapping
  • Credibility gaps and entry barriers
  • Offer positioning and first-customer strategy
  • Phased investment and measurable milestones
04

Fractional Growth Leadership

Senior leadership when the organization needs executive ownership but not yet a permanent full-time hire.

  • Pipeline quality and opportunity governance
  • Executive customer strategy
  • Capture discipline and decision cadence
  • Growth-team leadership and accountability
  • Board and CEO reporting
05

Post-Award Transition

Temporary executive support to convert the promise sold into a governable, executable program.

  • Transition and mobilization planning
  • Decision rights and governance
  • Partner integration and accountability
  • Staffing, risk, and customer alignment
  • PMO startup and executive reporting
06

Program or Pursuit Recovery

A direct diagnosis when momentum, ownership, performance, or customer confidence is deteriorating.

  • Root-cause and stakeholder assessment
  • Assumption, ownership, and cadence reset
  • Partner and governance intervention
  • Customer-confidence recovery
  • Executive corrective-action plan
