Engagement Models

Bring in the level of leadership the situation requires.

Engagements are selective, confidential, and structured around a defined decision, opportunity, or execution challenge.

Focused Review

Executive Opportunity Assessment

A concentrated independent review, typically used before major pursuit investment, market entry, acquisition-related growth decisions, or executive approval gates.

Best for: bid/no-bid, external challenge, board or investor confidence.

Embedded Leadership

Capture Executive

Direct leadership or senior advisory support for a strategic pursuit from qualification through teaming, positioning, proposal, and award.

Best for: opportunities too important for fragmented ownership.

Ongoing Advisory

Fractional Growth Executive

Recurring executive support to shape pipeline, operating rhythm, partnerships, market choices, and pursuit discipline without adding a permanent executive role.

Best for: scaling firms, new business units, investor-backed companies.

Award to Execution

Post-Award Transition

Temporary executive support through contract startup, governance, partner integration, customer alignment, PMO establishment, and early delivery stabilization.

Best for: wins larger or more complex than the existing delivery model.

Corrective Intervention

Program or Pursuit Recovery

Independent diagnosis and focused intervention where performance, decision quality, team alignment, or customer confidence has deteriorated.

Best for: drift, missed milestones, executive concern, partner friction.

Executive Counsel

CEO and Board Advisory

Confidential counsel on growth choices, major opportunities, partner strategy, organizational readiness, and the tradeoffs hidden by internal consensus.

Best for: consequential decisions requiring an outside operating perspective.

Operating Principle

Advice should improve the decision and the actions that follow.

Lochlann does not add process for its own sake. Scope, cadence, and deliverables are matched to the decision at hand. Senior attention remains senior attention.
