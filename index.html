The Lochlann Decision Framework

Five realities govern every consequential growth decision.

The framework is deliberately simple. It forces leadership teams to test the opportunity against the factors most likely to determine both award and execution.

Customer

Need, authority, funding, access, evaluation behavior, and trust.

Competition

Position, incumbency, discriminators, likely moves, and comparative risk.

Partnership

Roles, incentives, capability gaps, approvals, and conflict exposure.

Execution

Solution maturity, staffing, price, transition, governance, and delivery readiness.

Leadership

Ownership, decision rights, investment discipline, and executive alignment.
