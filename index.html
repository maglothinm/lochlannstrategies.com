Representative Decision Patterns

Clearer choices before momentum becomes sunk cost.

The work begins with the decision and the evidence required to make it, rather than a predetermined consulting package.

Strategic pursuit

Challenge the investment before the bid machine takes over.

Test customer demand, access, competition, discriminators, partner logic, price posture, internal ownership, and delivery exposure.

Decision output Pursue, do not pursue, or proceed only under defined conditions.
Market entry

Replace broad market ambition with a credible first route to revenue.

Map the buyer, sponsor, funding path, channel, partner, credibility gaps, first-customer offer, and staged investment gates.

Decision output A sequenced route with owners, milestones, and explicit stop conditions.
Award to execution

Convert what was sold into an operating model leadership can govern.

Establish decision rights, staffing logic, partner accountability, risk controls, customer cadence, and executive reporting.

Decision output A controlled startup that protects confidence and reduces execution debt.
